## Empirical lessons banked in the rig

- macOS `/var` symlink makes spawned checks' `import.meta.url === argv[1]` main-guards
  no-op SILENTLY (exit 0, no output) — every rig temp path is realpath'd, and `runCheck`
  maps no-output to a loud -1.
- Ambient `OS_RECORD_DIR`/`OS_DB`/`OS_DROPS` are stripped from every child env — a redirected
  parent can never leak the real record into a child.
